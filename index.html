Deeply grateful to Prof. Sylvia Kwok for her guidance throughout my PhD journey. Her expertise in positive psychology, project implementation, and rigorous research approach have profoundly shaped my academic work.

Research

Digital Media & Mental Health

Correlations between digital platform use and emotional well-being in adolescents, using quantitative surveys and longitudinal data analysis.

Media Literacy Education

Developing and evaluating interventions to enhance critical thinking about online content, in collaboration with Prof. Sylvia Kwok.

Youth Online Behavior

Patterns of social media engagement, misinformation exposure, and prosocial behavior among young digital natives.

Key Projects

Media Literacy Training for At-Risk Youth in Hong Kong (2022–2024)

Role: Lead Researcher (PhD Project) | Supervisor: Prof. Sylvia Kwok

Funded by the City University of Hong Kong PhD Research Grant. Outcomes include 2 peer-reviewed papers and an intervention toolkit for schools.

Social Media Use and Academic Performance: A Cross-Cultural Study (2023–Present)

Role: Co-Investigator

Comparative analysis across Hong Kong, Mainland China, and Singapore, exploring the relationship between social media habits and educational outcomes.
